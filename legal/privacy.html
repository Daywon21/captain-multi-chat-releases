Captain Multi Chat — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026 · Product: Captain Multi Chat (OBS dock + local backend)

Captain Multi Chat is desktop software that runs on your computer. It connects your streaming platform accounts so chat can appear in one OBS dock. This policy describes how the Facebook / Meta integration handles data when you use Connect Facebook.

Who controls the data

You run the app locally. Platform tokens and settings are stored on your machine in an encrypted vault under your user profile. We do not operate a Captain Multi Chat cloud account that receives your Facebook messages.

Data we access (Facebook / Meta)

When you authorize Facebook Login for Captain Multi Chat, the app may access:

  • Your Facebook user id and display name (to show who is connected)
  • The list of Facebook Pages you manage (so you can pick which Page to use)
  • Page access tokens needed to attach to that Page’s Live video
  • Live video comments on the Page Live you are streaming (to show and send chat in the dock)

The app requests Page-related permissions only (for example pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement, pages_manage_engagement, pages_read_user_content). It does not need your email, friends list, photos, or birthday.

How data is used

  • Show unified chat in OBS while you are live
  • Send chat you type from the dock to your Page Live
  • Remember which Page you selected until you change it or log out

We do not sell Facebook data. We do not use it for advertising.

Storage and retention

  • Tokens and Page selection stay on your PC until you log out of Facebook in Settings or uninstall/clear the app data folder
  • Chat lines shown in the dock are kept in memory (and optional local UI buffers) for the session — not uploaded to a Captain Multi Chat server

Third parties

Facebook/Meta processes authentication and Graph/Live APIs under Meta’s terms. See Meta’s policies at facebook.com/policy.php.

Children

Captain Multi Chat is intended for streamers and is not directed at children under 13.

Your choices

  • Log out of Facebook inside Captain Multi Chat Settings
  • Remove the app from your Facebook account: facebook.com/settings → Apps and websites
  • Request deletion of local app data by deleting the Captain Multi Chat data folder on your PC (see the User Data Deletion page)

Contact

Questions about this policy or Facebook data use: open an issue on the public releases repository Daywon21/captain-multi-chat-releases or contact the app publisher via that repository.
